About Sunrise Cafe

Started in 2015, Sunrise Cafe is a neighborhood spot for specialty coffee and small-batch pastries. We source ethically and roast in small batches to bring out the best flavor in every cup.

  • Locally roasted beans
  • Scratch-baked pastries
  • Cozy indoor + sunny outdoor seating
